HTML5comes with a bunch of new elements and more will be recommended in the future. However, there are some elements that might be a little confusing in their implementation, including the following two new elements: <article> and <section>.

Some most frequently asked question I found in the forums are: In what circumstances should we use these elements? And similarly How do we use this elements, correctly?
